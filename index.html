About Me

Software Engineering Student

Ever since I was a child, I've had a strong curiosity for how computers work. I spent countless hours exploring technology, experimenting with software, and teaching myself the basics of programming. That early fascination only grew stronger over time and became a defining part of who I am.

I later attended SUGS Rade Jovcevski - "Korcagin" High School, where I built a solid academic foundation and continued to nurture my interest in technology. This passion naturally led me to pursue higher education at the Faculty of Computer Science and Engineering (FINKI), where I am currently studying Software Engineering.

I'm especially passionate about front-end development. I enjoy creating visually engaging and user-friendly interfaces that combine both creativity and logical thinking. Building websites that not only look good but also solve real problems is something I truly find fulfilling.

Outside of the digital world, I have a big love for animals, and I'm also deeply fascinated by real estate and architecture. I find joy in observing how both physical and virtual spaces are designed to meet human needs.

I'm someone who is constantly curious and motivated to learn. I believe that the tech world offers limitless opportunities to grow, create, and make a real impact — and I'm excited to be part of it.
